Initial planning for Weather Site

Home

Purpose Statement

To provide every citizen with up to date weather information so they can plan their day to day activities with ease and an assurance that they are ready for all that nature may throw at them.

We hope to become the leading weather site and weather app to promote our stations relevance as the best and most reliable in the country.

Audience Description

This site is for all persons that have access to the internet, mainly those from ages sixteen and up who are usually interested in the weather conditions. They can use our site to plan for things like what to wear, when to be driving, and the casual outing trip with friends, family, etc. The information we provide will be priceless and easily accessible that even those that believe they don’t understand technology will be able to find what they are looking for through smart phone, tablet, or computer.
