Visitor Analysis

1. What are their demographics? Age, Income, Education, Geographic Location.

This website is one that would be more appealing to persons of 14 and over, mainly those that like sports. It doesn’t matter the education or income, just internet access is necessary to be able to visit this site mobily.

2. How did the site meet the needs of the visitor (be specific in terms of "Less is More)?

The site give two things that are most important to visitors, scores and game/match schedules with date and times. This makes life easy and simple for the user and this mobile site shows how "less" is much more appealing.

3. Describe how the content was organized to facilitate it being found.

Menu bar at top to select desired sport, to news of the day on front of page, and sport matchup of the day including time viewing on espn's channel. Also scores tab for quick review.

4. What did the site to make the mobile experience enjoyable (or at least not painful)?

Simple and easy to find what I was looking for, scores of all games at the end of the day. I didn't have to sort through tons of material. I pressed two tabs and got what I was looking for.

5. Did the site seem to facilitate people in a mobile environment (Think about the descriptions in the Visitor Considerations reading)? If "Yes", how?

Yes, this site has adapted well to mobile access especially since users are now able to watch games from phones rather than needing a laptop or cable t.v. This allows full access and less material distraction from the original site.
